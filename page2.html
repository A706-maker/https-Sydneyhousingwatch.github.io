The First-home buyer Crisis

Income vs. Housing Cost

In March 2002, the cost of an average house in Sydney was 8.3 times that of the average annual full time wage. While this is of course expensive, the rate which it has been raised to as of 2024 makes it look incredibly cheap. In June 2024, the price of that average dwelling in Sydney is now equivalent to 14.4 years earning the average wage in NSW. To put it more simply, residents in Sydney need to pay $615,000 more in today’s dollars than 20years ago
